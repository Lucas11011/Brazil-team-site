About the Team

Welcome to my website for the Brazilian National Soccer team! The Brazil national soccer team represents Brazil in international men's soccer. It is the most successful national team in the FIFA World Cup, with five championship titles.

Achievements

  • FIFA World Cup Champions: 1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002
  • Copa América Champions: 1919, 1922, 1949, 1989, 1997, 1999, 2004, 2007, 2019
  • FIFA Confederations Cup Champions: 1997, 2005, 2009, 2013
